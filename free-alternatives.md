# Free Alternatives to Paid Software

Comprehensive collection of free and open-source alternatives to popular paid productivity software and services.

Why pay when excellent free alternatives exist? These tools offer comparable or superior functionality without the cost.

## Office Suites

- [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org) - Full-featured, open-source office suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint alternative).
- [Apache OpenOffice](https://www.openoffice.org) - Free office productivity suite with word processor, spreadsheet, and presentation tools.
- [OnlyOffice](https://www.onlyoffice.com) - Free office suite with excellent Microsoft Office compatibility.
- [Google Workspace](https://workspace.google.com) - Free online office suite (Docs, Sheets, Slides).
- [Zoho Office Suite](https://www.zoho.com/docs) - Comprehensive free online office applications.
- [WPS Office](https://www.wps.com) - Free office suite with Microsoft Office compatibility.
- [Calligra Suite](https://calligra.org) - Free office and graphic art suite for Linux.
- [SoftMaker FreeOffice](https://www.freeoffice.com) - Free alternative to Microsoft Office with excellent compatibility.
