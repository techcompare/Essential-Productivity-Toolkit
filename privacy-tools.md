# Privacy & Ad-Blocking Tools

Essential tools and resources for protecting your privacy, blocking ads, preventing tracking, and maintaining digital security.

Privacy is a fundamental right in the digital age. These tools help you browse safely, block intrusive ads, and protect your personal data.

## Ad Blockers

- [uBlock Origin](https://ublockorigin.com) - Efficient, open-source ad blocker and content filter.
- [AdGuard](https://adguard.com) - Comprehensive ad blocking for browsers and entire systems.
- [AdBlock Plus](https://adblockplus.org) - Popular open-source ad blocker with customizable filters.
- [Brave Browser](https://brave.com) - Built-in ad and tracker blocking in privacy-focused browser.
- [Pi-hole](https://pi-hole.net) - Network-wide ad blocking via DNS on Raspberry Pi or VM.
- [AdAway](https://adaway.org) - Open-source ad blocker for Android using hosts file.
- [Blokada](https://blokada.org) - Free, open-source ad blocker for Android and iOS.
- [NextDNS](https://nextdns.io) - Cloud-based DNS filtering for ads and trackers.

## Anti-Tracking Tools
