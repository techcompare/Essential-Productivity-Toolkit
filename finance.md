This section focuses on financial management tools and resources that help track spending, budget effectively, and build wealth.

Financial productivity means managing money intentionally, reducing financial stress, and making informed decisions about spending and saving.

## Resources

- [Mint](https://mint.intuit.com) - Free budgeting app that tracks expenses, bills, and credit score in one place.
- [YNAB (You Need A Budget)](https://www.youneedabudget.com) - Zero-based budgeting app that helps you give every dollar a job.
- [Personal Capital](https://www.personalcapital.com) - Financial planning and wealth management with investment tracking.
- [PocketGuard](https://pocketguard.com) - Budget app that shows how much you have available to spend after bills and savings.
- [EveryDollar](https://www.everydollar.com) - Simple budgeting tool based on Dave Ramsey's baby steps method.
- [Goodbudget](https://goodbudget.com) - Envelope budgeting system for managing household finances together.
- [Simplifi by Quicken](https://www.quicken.com/simplifi) - Personal finance app for budgeting, tracking spending, and planning.
- [Expensify](https://www.expensify.com) - Expense tracking and report generation for business and personal use.
- [Splitwise](https://www.splitwise.com) - Track shared expenses and bills with roommates, partners, or groups.
- [Wave](https://www.waveapps.com) - Free accounting, invoicing, and receipt scanning for small businesses.
- [QuickBooks](https://quickbooks.intuit.com) - Comprehensive accounting software for small business financial management.
- [FreshBooks](https://www.freshbooks.com) - Cloud accounting software for invoicing, expenses, and time tracking.
- [Xero](https://www.xero.com) - Online accounting software for small businesses with bank reconciliation.
- [Toshl Finance](https://toshl.com) - Personal finance app with expense tracking, budgets, and financial insights.
- [Moneydance](https://moneydance.com) - Personal finance software with investment tracking and bill management.
- [Banktivity](https://www.iggsoftware.com/banktivity) - Personal finance manager for Mac and iOS with forecasting.
- [MoneyWiz](https://wiz.money) - Personal finance app with multi-currency support and investment tracking.
- [Spendee](https://www.spendee.com) - Budget and expense tracker with shared wallets and cash flow insights.
- [Honeydue](https://www.honeydue.com) - Finance app for couples to manage money and bills together.
- [Acorns](https://www.acorns.com) - Automated investing app that rounds up purchases and invests spare change.
- [Robinhood](https://robinhood.com) - Commission-free trading platform for stocks, ETFs, and cryptocurrency.
- [M1 Finance](https://www.m1finance.com) - Automated investing with customizable portfolios and fractional shares.
- [Betterment](https://www.betterment.com) - Robo-advisor for automated investing and retirement planning.
- [Wealthfront](https://www.wealthfront.com) - Automated investment management with tax-loss harvesting.
- [Vanguard](https://investor.vanguard.com) - Low-cost index funds and investment platform for long-term wealth building.
- [Credit Karma](https://www.creditkarma.com) - Free credit score monitoring and personalized financial recommendations.
- [NerdWallet](https://www.nerdwallet.com) - Personal finance comparison site for credit cards, banking, and investing.
- [Tiller Money](https://www.tillerhq.com) - Automated spreadsheet tracking for personal finance with customizable templates.
- [Lunch Money](https://lunchmoney.app) - Personal finance and budgeting app designed for developers and power users.
- [Actual Budget](https://actualbudget.com) - Open-source budgeting tool with local-first data and privacy focus.
- [Buxfer](https://www.buxfer.com) - Personal finance software for budgeting, forecasting, and investment tracking.
- [MoneyPatrol](https://moneypatrol.com) - Personal finance software with budgeting, bill reminders, and reports.
- [The Richest Man in Babylon](https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/1052.The_Richest_Man_in_Babylon) - Classic book on financial wisdom and wealth-building principles.
- [I Will Teach You to Be Rich](https://www.iwillteachyoutoberich.com) - Six-week program for personal finance optimization.
- [Your Money or Your Life](https://yourmoneyoryourlife.com) - Transforming relationship with money and achieving financial independence.
