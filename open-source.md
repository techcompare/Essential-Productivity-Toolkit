# Open Source Productivity Software

Comprehensive collection of open-source productivity tools that respect your freedom, privacy, and provide full transparency.

Open source means you own your tools, your data, and your workflow. These applications are free, customizable, and community-driven.

## Office & Documents

- [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org) - Complete office suite with writer, spreadsheet, and presentation tools.
- [Apache OpenOffice](https://www.openoffice.org) - Open-source office productivity suite.
- [OnlyOffice](https://www.onlyoffice.com) - Office suite with excellent MS Office compatibility.
- [Calligra Suite](https://calligra.org) - Integrated office and graphic art suite.
- [AbiWord](https://www.abisource.com) - Lightweight open-source word processor.
- [Gnumeric](http://www.gnumeric.org) - Fast, accurate spreadsheet application.
- [TeX/LaTeX](https://www.latex-project.org) - Document preparation system for high-quality typesetting.
- [LyX](https://www.lyx.org) - Document processor combining LaTeX power with GUI ease.
