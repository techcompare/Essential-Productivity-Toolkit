
This section emphasizes sustainable productivity and mental wellbeing.

It includes resources that promote balance, prevent burnout, and support healthy work habits alongside productivity.

## Resources

- [Headspace](https://www.headspace.com) - Meditation and mindfulness app for mental health.
- [Calm](https://www.calm.com) - App for sleep, meditation, and relaxation.
- [Insight Timer](https://insighttimer.com) - Free meditation app with thousands of guided practices.
- [Streaks](https://streaksapp.com) - Habit tracker to build positive daily routines.
- [Loop Habit Tracker](https://github.com/iSoron/uhabits) - Open-source habit tracker for Android.
- [Forest](https://www.forestapp.cc) - Stay focused while planting real trees through the app.
- [Digital Minimalism by Cal Newport](https://www.calnewport.com/books/digital-minimalism) - Book about intentional technology use.
- [Atomic Habits by James Clear](https://jamesclear.com/atomic-habits) - Guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones.
- [f.lux](https://justgetflux.com) - Adjusts screen color temperature to reduce eye strain.
- [Stand Up! The Work Break Timer](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/stand-up-the-work-break-timer/id828244687) - Reminds you to take regular breaks while working.
