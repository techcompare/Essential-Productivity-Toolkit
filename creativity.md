This section focuses on tools and practices that support creativity, innovation, and creative problem-solving.

Creative productivity involves capturing ideas, exploring new perspectives, and building systems that support creative work and innovation.

## Resources

- [Notion](https://www.notion.so) - Flexible workspace for organizing creative projects, mood boards, and ideas.
- [Miro](https://miro.com) - Online collaborative whiteboard for brainstorming and visual thinking.
- [Mural](https://www.mural.co) - Digital workspace for visual collaboration with design thinking frameworks.
- [Figma](https://www.figma.com) - Collaborative design tool for interface design, prototyping, and graphics.
- [Sketch](https://www.sketch.com) - Vector graphics editor for UI/UX design on macOS.
- [Adobe Creative Cloud](https://www.adobe.com/creativecloud.html) - Suite of creative apps including Photoshop, Illustrator, and Premiere Pro.
- [Canva](https://www.canva.com) - Graphic design platform for creating professional visuals without design skills.
- [Procreate](https://procreate.art) - Digital painting and illustration app for iPad with powerful brush engine.
- [Affinity Designer](https://affinity.serif.com/designer) - Professional vector graphics software with one-time purchase.
- [Affinity Photo](https://affinity.serif.com/photo) - Professional photo editing software alternative to Photoshop.
- [GIMP](https://www.gimp.org) - Free and open-source image editor with advanced features.
- [Inkscape](https://inkscape.org) - Free vector graphics editor for illustrations and designs.
- [Blender](https://www.blender.org) - Open-source 3D creation suite for modeling, animation, and rendering.
- [DaVinci Resolve](https://www.blackmagicdesign.com/products/davinciresolve) - Professional video editing and color grading software.
- [Final Cut Pro](https://www.apple.com/final-cut-pro) - Professional video editing application for macOS.
- [Logic Pro](https://www.apple.com/logic-pro) - Professional music production software for macOS.
- [Ableton Live](https://www.ableton.com/live) - Digital audio workstation for music creation and live performance.
- [GarageBand](https://www.apple.com/mac/garageband) - Free music creation software for Mac and iOS devices.
- [Audacity](https://www.audacityteam.org) - Free, open-source audio editing and recording software.
- [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) - Knowledge base for connecting ideas and building creative thought networks.
- [Roam Research](https://roamresearch.com) - Note-taking tool for networked thought and idea connection.
- [Craft](https://www.craft.do) - Beautiful document editor for notes, tasks, and creative writing.
- [Ulysses](https://ulysses.app) - Writing app for creative writers with distraction-free focus mode.
- [Scrivener](https://www.literatureandlatte.com/scrivener) - Powerful writing tool for long-form content like novels and research papers.
- [Bear](https://bear.app) - Beautiful, flexible writing app for notes and creative prose on Apple devices.
- [iA Writer](https://ia.net/writer) - Minimalist writing app with focus mode and markdown support.
- [Hemingway Editor](https://hemingwayapp.com) - Writing tool that improves clarity and readability.
- [MindMeister](https://www.mindmeister.com) - Online mind mapping tool for brainstorming and idea organization.

- [Are.na](https://www.are.na) - Platform for collecting and connecting ideas collaboratively.
- [Evernote](https://evernote.com) - Note-taking app with web clipper for capturing creative inspiration.
- [Pocket](https://getpocket.com) - Save articles, videos, and creative content to review later.
- [Raindrop.io](https://raindrop.io) - Bookmark manager for organizing inspiration and resources.
- [The Artist's Way](https://juliacameronlive.com/the-artists-way) - Book on creative recovery and unblocking creative potential.
- [Steal Like an Artist](https://austinkleon.com/steal) - Book on embracing influence and finding your creative voice.
- [Big Magic](https://www.elizabethgilbert.com/big-magic) - Book about living creatively beyond fear and embracing curiosity.
