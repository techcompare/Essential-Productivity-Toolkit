
# Awesome Productivity Toolkit [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated collection of essential tools, resources, and systems to improve focus, organization, and everyday productivity.

Designed for **students, professionals, creators, and lifelong learners** who want practical, high-quality productivity resources without noise or clutter.

## Planning & Organization

Tools and systems to plan better and stay organized.

- [Notion](https://www.notion.so) - All-in-one workspace for notes, tasks, databases, and collaboration.
- [Todoist](https://todoist.com) - Task manager with natural language processing and cross-platform sync.
- [Trello](https://trello.com) - Visual project management using Kanban boards.
- [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) - Knowledge base and note-taking app that works on local Markdown files.
- [Bullet Journal Method](https://bulletjournal.com) - Analog productivity system for mindful organization.
- [Getting Things Done (GTD)](https://gettingthingsdone.com) - Time management method to capture and organize tasks systematically.
- [Timeblocking](https://todoist.com/productivity-methods/time-blocking) - Schedule technique that dedicates specific time blocks to tasks.
- [Airtable](https://airtable.com) - Spreadsheet-database hybrid for organizing anything.
- [ClickUp](https://clickup.com) - Project management platform with docs, goals, and time tracking.

## Focus & Deep Work

Resources to reduce distractions and improve concentration.

- [Freedom](https://freedom.to) - Block distracting websites and apps across all your devices.
- [Cold Turkey](https://getcoldturkey.com) - Powerful distraction blocker for Windows and macOS.
- [Forest](https://www.forestapp.cc) - Gamified focus timer that grows virtual trees while you work.
- [Deep Work by Cal Newport](https://www.calnewport.com/books/deep-work) - Book about the value of focused work in a distracted world.
- [Pomodoro Technique](https://francescocirillo.com/pages/pomodoro-technique) - Time management method using 25-minute focused intervals.
- [Brain.fm](https://brain.fm) - Functional music designed to improve focus and productivity.
- [Toggl Track](https://toggl.com/track) - Simple time tracking tool to understand where your time goes.
- [RescueTime](https://www.rescuetime.com) - Automatic time tracking and productivity insights.
- [Be Focused](https://xwavesoft.com/be-focused-pro-for-iphone-ipad-mac-os-x.html) - Pomodoro timer with task management integration.

## Tools & Apps

Practical tools that support everyday productivity.

- [Notion](https://www.notion.so) - Versatile workspace combining notes, databases, and project management.
- [Evernote](https://evernote.com) - Note-taking app with powerful search and organization features.
- [Zapier](https://zapier.com) - Automation platform connecting thousands of apps without code.
- [IFTTT](https://ifttt.com) - Simple automation tool for connecting apps and devices.
- [Alfred](https://www.alfredapp.com) - Productivity app for macOS with workflows and snippets.
- [Raycast](https://www.raycast.com) - Extendable launcher for macOS with built-in productivity tools.
- [1Password](https://1password.com) - Password manager for secure credential storage and sharing.
- [Pocket](https://getpocket.com) - Save articles and videos to read or watch later.
- [Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com) - Writing assistant for grammar, spelling, and clarity.
- [TextExpander](https://textexpander.com) - Snippet expansion tool to automate repetitive typing.

Resources for continuous improvement and skill building.

- [edX](https://www.edx.org) - Free online courses from world-class institutions.
- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org) - Free educational platform covering math, science, and more.
- [Anki](https://apps.ankiweb.net) - Spaced repetition flashcard app for effective memorization.
- [Blinkist](https://www.blinkist.com) - Key insights from nonfiction books in 15-minute reads.
- [Goodreads](https://www.goodreads.com) - Track reading progress and discover new books.
- [Feynman Technique](https://fs.blog/feynman-technique) - Learning method based on explaining concepts simply.
- [Skillshare](https://www.skillshare.com) - Online learning community for creative and practical skills.
- [Udemy](https://www.udemy.com) - Marketplace for learning and teaching online courses.
- [Habitica](https://habitica.com) - Gamify your life by turning tasks and habits into an RPG.

Productivity resources for professional life.

## Wellbeing & Balance

Sustainable productivity without burnout.

- [Headspace](https://www.headspace.com) - Meditation and mindfulness app for mental health.
- [Calm](https://www.calm.com) - App for sleep, meditation, and relaxation.
- [Insight Timer](https://insighttimer.com) - Free meditation app with thousands of guided practices.
- [Streaks](https://streaksapp.com) - Habit tracker to build positive daily routines.
- [Loop Habit Tracker](https://github.com/iSoron/uhabits) - Open-source habit tracker for Android.
- [Forest](https://www.forestapp.cc) - Stay focused while planting real trees through the app.
- [Digital Minimalism by Cal Newport](https://www.calnewport.com/books/digital-minimalism) - Book about intentional technology use.
- [Atomic Habits by James Clear](https://jamesclear.com/atomic-habits) - Guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones.
- [f.lux](https://justgetflux.com) - Adjusts screen color temperature to reduce eye strain.
- [Stand Up! The Work Break Timer](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/stand-up-the-work-break-timer/id828244687) - Reminds you to take regular breaks while working.
